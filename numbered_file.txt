1  = You have power over your mind - not outside events. Realize this, and you will find strength.
2  = The universe is change; our life is what our thoughts make it.
3  = Waste no more time arguing about what a good man should be. Be one.
4  = Very little is needed to make a happy life; it is all within yourself, in your way of thinking.
5  = If it is not right do not do it; if it is not true do not say it.
6  = Love nothing but that which comes to you woven in the pattern of your destiny. For what could more aptly fit your needs?
7  = It is not death that a man should fear, but he should fear never beginning to live.
8  = The object of life is not to be on the side of the majority, but to escape finding oneself in the ranks of the insane.
9  = The soul becomes dyed with the color of its thoughts.
